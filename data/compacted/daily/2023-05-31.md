- **Natural Consequences Examples**: Forget jacket (feel cold), don't eat dinner (hunger later), don't care for toys (damage/break), don't pack school bag (late for school), insufficient sleep (tired/grumpy), don't study (poor test grades). Must not pose serious danger.
