## Gardening: Cucumber Harvest and Preservation
**Session:** 743f03a1_abs_2  
**Garden Status:** 3 cucumber plants producing abundant daily harvest.  
**Storage Guidelines:** Refrigerate below 40°F (4°C); breathable containers (paper bag, mesh bag, ventilated plastic); store upright; keep dry (do not wash before storing); cucumber keeper containers; freezing option (blanch 3-5 minutes).  
**Storage Durations:** Slicing cucumbers 7-10 days; pickling cucumbers 10-14 days; English cucumbers 10-14 days; mini cucumbers 5-7 days.  
**Recipe Categories:** Cucumber Salad with Feta and Mint; Cucumber Raita; Cucumber and Smoked Salmon Rolls; Cucumber and Tomato Salad with Burrata; Cucumber and Avocado Soup; Cucumber Kimchi; Cucumber and Hummus Wrap; Cucumber and Feta Phyllo Triangles; Cucumber and Mint Juice; Cucumber Pickles.  
**Dill Pickle Recipe (Lacto-Fermentation):**  
- **Full Batch:** 4-6 pickling cucumbers (1-2 lbs), 1/4 cup kosher salt, 1/4 cup granulated sugar, 1/2 cup water, 1/4 cup white vinegar, 2 tbsp dried dill weed, 1 tsp whole black peppercorns, 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional). Ferment 3-5 days at 70-75°F (21-24°C).  
- **Small Batch (1-2 jars):** 1-2 cups sliced cucumbers (1/2-1 lb), 1/8 cup kosher salt, 1/8 cup sugar, 1/4 cup water, 1/8 cup white vinegar, 1 tsp dried dill, 1/2 tsp peppercorns, 1/8 tsp red pepper flakes.  
**Lacto-Fermentation Process:** Lactic acid bacteria (Lactobacillus plantarum) feed on sugars, produce lactic acid byproduct, acidifies environment (lowers pH), creates anaerobic conditions, produces antimicrobial compounds, enhances crunch and tangy flavor.  
**Salt Specifications:** Kosher salt preferred (coarser texture, less refined, no iodine/anti-caking agents) vs table salt (denser, requires less volume, may contain additives affecting flavor/texture).
