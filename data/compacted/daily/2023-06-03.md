- **Cilantro Lime Rice:** White or brown rice cooked with chopped cilantro, lime juice, olive oil.

**Beverage:** Cucumber Lime Refresher with Korean Chili Flake-Infused Simple Syrup.

**Korean Chili Flake-Infused Simple Syrup Recipe:**
- 1 cup granulated sugar.
- 1 cup water.
- 1-2 teaspoons Korean chili flakes (gochugaru).
- Preparation: Heat sugar/water until dissolved, remove from heat, add chili flakes, steep 30+ minutes, strain through fine-mesh sieve, cool, refrigerate up to 2 weeks.
- Optional additions: Ginger slice or cinnamon stick during steeping.
- Refresher assembly: Muddle sliced cucumber and lime juice, add syrup and sparkling water, garnish with cucumber slice and lime wheel.
