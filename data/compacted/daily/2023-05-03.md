## Session c3757c5b: Spain Travel Planning & Cultural Research

**Trip Context:** Planning trip to Spain, residing in US for 3 years, seeking basic Spanish phrases and cultural customs for traveler interactions.

**Language Learning Resources Identified:**
- **Apps:** Duolingo (free, gamified), Babbel (paid, conversational, travel module, speech recognition), Rosetta Stone (travel module, speech recognition), Anki (spaced repetition flashcards), Memrise (community-driven, user-generated), TripLingo (travel-focused, phrasebooks, Spain-specific module), HelloTalk (native speaker exchange), iTalki (online teachers).
- **Key Phrases:** "Hola," "Buenos días/tardes/noches," "¿Dónde está...?," "¿Cuánto cuesta?," "Un billete de ida y vuelta, por favor," "Un café, por favor," "Un vaso de agua, por favor."

**Cultural Norms & Etiquette:**
- **Greetings:** Firm handshake for first meetings/formal; "dos besos" (two cheek kisses) for friends/casual (not expected from foreigners). Formal titles: "Señor" (Mr.), "Señora" (Mrs./Ms.). Use "tú" (informal) with younger people.
- **Time & Punctuality:** "La hora española" (Spanish time) allows 10-15 minutes lateness in social situations. Siesta observed (early afternoon shop closures). Lunch (la comida) 2:00-4:00 pm; dinner (la cena) 9:00-11:00 pm. Relationships prioritized over strict schedules.
- **Social Norms:** Direct eye contact = respect. Physical touch common. Tipping 5-10% in restaurants/bars. Dress modestly for churches. Respect regional differences.

**Alhambra, Granada:**
- 14th-century palace/fortress complex, Islamic architecture, UNESCO site.
- **Sections:** Alcazaba (fortress), Generalife (summer palace/gardens), Palacio Nazarí (Patio de los Arrayanes, Salón de Embajadores), Patio de la Acequia, Patio de los Leones.
- **Night Visits:** "Visita Nocturna" (April-October), illuminated complex, guided tours, special events.
- **Logistics:** Book tickets days in advance; allow half-day; wear comfortable shoes/modest dress; expect crowds; arrive early for serene atmosphere.
