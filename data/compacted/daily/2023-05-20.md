### Session `answer_c2204106_2` – Plant Care & Gardening

**Peace Lily (Spathiphyllum) Care:**  
- **Acquisition:** Purchased from nursery 2 weeks ago alongside a succulent; currently experiencing normal leaf loss during environmental adjustment.  
- **Lighting:** Bright, indirect light; ideal placement east- or west-facing windows; avoid direct sunlight (leaf scorch) and low light (prevents blooming).  
- **Watering:** Overwatering is primary cause of death; check soil moisture by inserting finger to first knuckle; water only when dry; ensure pot drains thoroughly to prevent waterlogged soil. Frequency: every 7–10 days (spring/summer), every 10–14 days (fall/winter); top 1–2 inches must feel dry.  
- **Humidity:** Prefers humid environment; adaptable to average 40–50% humidity; mist 1–2 times weekly (morning only, room-temperature water); avoid over-misting to prevent fungal disease and root rot; do not mist soil or crown (where leaves meet stem).  
- **Temperature:** Current range 65°F–70°F (18°C–21°C) is acceptable; ideal range 68°F–72°F (20°C–22°C); acceptable range 65°F–75°F (18°C–24°C); avoid below 55°F (13°C) or above 85°F (29°C); keep away from drafts, vents, fireplaces.  
- **Fertilization:** Use balanced water-soluble fertilizer N-P-K 20-20-20 (or 20-15-15 with higher nitrogen); dilute to half recommended strength; apply every 1–2 weeks during growing season (spring/summer), monthly during dormancy (fall/winter). User currently uses nursery-recommended water-fertilizer mixture; advised to verify if balanced or bloom-boosting (e.g., 10-20-10 high-phosphorus) and ensure proper dilution to avoid root burn.  
- **Potting Mix:** Well-draining mix designed for tropical plants or orchids.  
- **Pruning:** Remove dead/dying leaves and flowers with clean sharp scissors to prevent disease spread.  
- **Repotting:** Only when outgrown or nutrient deficient; perform in spring/summer using slightly larger pot and fresh mix.  
- **Pest Control:** Monitor for spider mites, mealybugs, scale; treat with insecticidal soap or neem oil.  
- **Tracking:** User utilizes plant tracking app for watering/fertilization reminders.
