**HR Analytics & Turnover Calculations**
- **Formula**: Turnover rate = (Number of employees who left / Average number of employees) × 100%.
- **Average calculation**: (Beginning headcount + Ending headcount) / 2.
- **Example 1**: 177 left, average 700 employees = 25.29% turnover rate.
- **Example 2**: January 2022: 741 employees, December 2022: 809 employees, 169 left. Average: (741+809)/2 = 775. Turnover rate: 21.81%.
- **Benchmarking**: No universal "best" rate; varies by industry (retail/hospitality higher); low turnover indicates satisfaction but possible stagnation; high turnover indicates retention problems.
