* This method can lead to loss of information, so be careful not to remove too many instances.

* This method helps the model focus more on the minority class during training.

* Select the most difficult negative examples (i.e., those that are most similar to the positive class) and add them to the training set.
* This method helps the model learn to distinguish between the positive and negative classes more effectively.
